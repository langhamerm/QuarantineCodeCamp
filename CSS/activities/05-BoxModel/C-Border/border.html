The border-style Property

This property specifies what kind of border to display:

A 1px red dotted border.

A thick silver dashed border.

A 2px rounded solid border.

A 2px yellow double border.

A green groove border 2px wide on the top and bottom and 1px on the sides.

A magenta ridge border.

An inset border.

An outset border.

No border.

A hidden border.

A mixed border.
